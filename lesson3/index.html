Purpose

The purpose of the St. George Chamber of Commerce website is to represent the interests of over 1000 businesses and nonprofits who are directly impacted by the strength of the local economy. We want to help build our enconmy here in St. George by encouraging everyone to work togther and share ideas with one another. And this website will help with that.

Pages

This website will include a few different pages to help potential members understand what they have the opportunity of joining or learning about. We will include a Homepage that gives a brief explanation of our goal and some highlights. A Discover page information about the town. A Directory page to list organizations in the area. A Join page containing information about memberships and an application form. And lastly a Contact page to allow users to contact the officers and board of directors.
