Target Audience

Our target audience is local business owners and entrepreneurs who are looking to grow their businesses and connect with other like-minded individuals in the community.

Personas

Maria is a 40-year-old owner of a small boutique in the downtown area. She is passionate about her business and wants to see it thrive. She is looking for resources and support to help her grow her business and reach new customers.

David is a 35-year-old entrepreneur who recently started a tech company. He is new to the area and wants to connect with other business owners and learn more about the local business community.

Scenarios

  • Maria wants to attend a networking event to meet other business owners and potential customers.
  • David is looking for a mentor to help him navigate the challenges of starting a new business.
  • Maria wants to learn more about marketing strategies to attract more customers to her boutique.
  • David is interested in attending a workshop on how to secure funding for his startup.
  • Maria wants to collaborate with other local businesses on a joint marketing campaign.
